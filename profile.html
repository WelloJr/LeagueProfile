Luxanna Crownguard

The Lady of Luminosity

Biography

Born to the prestigious Crownguard family in Demacia, Luxanna “Lux” Crownguard was destined for greatness. As a child, she exhibited a remarkable talent for magic, an ability that is not only rare in Demacia but is also often viewed with suspicion and fear. Despite the potential danger, Lux embraced her powers and sought to use them for good, ultimately becoming a defender of her homeland.

Abilities

  • Light Binding: Lux releases a sphere of light that binds and deals damage to up to two enemy units.
  • Prismatic Barrier: Lux throws her wand and bends the light around any friendly target it touches, protecting them from damage.
  • Lucent Singularity: Lux creates a zone of light that slows enemies and can be detonated to deal damage.
  • Final Spark: After gathering energy, Lux fires a beam of light that deals damage to all enemies in a line. It also triggers her passive ability, Illumination, dealing bonus damage to affected enemies.

Role in Demacia

Lux has taken on a crucial role in the defense of Demacia, often working behind the scenes to protect her people. Her mastery of light magic, combined with her unwavering dedication to her country, makes her a formidable and respected figure among her peers and a beacon of hope for all of Demacia.
